Stroganoff

Sometimes you just need something that's quick and easy that everyone likes.

Ingredients

  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 lb ground beef (90% or more)
  • 1 lb pkg egg noodles
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp worcesstershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350Â°
  2. cook the egg noodles according the directions on the package
  3. while the noodles are boiling cook the meat in a large pan on the stove top, breaking it up into small pieces
  4. combine the other ingredients in a large bowl until mixture is consistent
  5. combine all the ingredients and mix until consistent
  6. poor mixture into an 9x13 baking dish or something similar
  7. bake in the oven for about 30 minutes until heated evenly throughout
