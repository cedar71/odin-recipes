Super Easy Delicious Fudge

This is recipe makes awesome fudge and it's so easy I could do it. There are no rare ingredients or special equipment required.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups chocoloate chips
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • dash of vanilla extract (optional)
  • pinch of salt (optional)

Steps

  1. melt butter and chocoloate chips, stirring frequently until completely melted
  2. stir in vanilla and salt
  3. add sweetened condensed milk
  4. stir until completely combined
  5. poor mixture into an 8x8 pan or something similar to cool
  6. once the fudge has cooled some, place the pan in the fridge to set up
